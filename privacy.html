Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all Fusion Add-Ins published by NCL, including Precise SVG Import and Merge Sketch Points.

Data Collection

Our add-ins do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing occurs entirely within your local Fusion installation. No information is sent to NCL or any third-party service.

Local Files

Our add-ins may create the following local files on your computer:

Precise SVG Import:

  • Settings file storing your preferences (DPI mode, origin mode, etc.)
  • Log files for troubleshooting purposes

Merge Sketch Points:

  • No files are created outside of Fusion's own data

These files remain on your computer and are never transmitted to any external service.

Third-Party Services

Our add-ins do not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Autodesk App Store

Our add-ins are distributed through the Autodesk App Store, which is operated by Autodesk, Inc. Your use of the Autodesk App Store is subject to Autodesk's own privacy policy. NCL does not receive any personal data from Autodesk as part of the purchase or download process beyond what is necessary to fulfill your order.

Children's Privacy

Our add-ins are not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@newtoncreativelabs.com.
